WELCOME from FUTURE

  • "Planet Travel Station is a Burmese Private agency that is responsible for science and technology related to air and space. The Space Age began in 1957 with the launch of the Soviet satellite Sputnik"
  • Planet Travel Station opened for business on Oct. 1, 2055. The agency was established to oversee Burmese space exploration and aeronautics research.

What We Are Offering

  • The primary mission of Planet Travel Station is to fulfill people's dreams of space travel and enable them to explore and expand their knowledge and experiences. Additionally, the agency plays a crucial role in supporting aspiring students who aspire to become engineers, scientists, and astronauts of the future. These young individuals will be the intrepid explorers who will continue to push the boundaries of exploration within our solar system and the broader universe.
  • Planet Travel Station has a rich tradition of investing in programs and activities that ignite the excitement of exploration in students, educators, families, and communities. The agency's commitment to inspiring the spirit of discovery is evident in its efforts to engage and captivate audiences of all ages.
  • One of Planet Travel Station's key initiatives involves providing specialized training to teachers. This training equips educators with innovative methods to teach subjects such as science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. By empowering teachers with these tools, the agency contributes to the development of a new generation of scientists and engineers, ensuring a brighter future for space exploration and scientific advancement.
